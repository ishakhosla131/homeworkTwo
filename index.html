About Me

Hey there! Thanks for visiting my site. I'm a New Jersey based self-taught artist. My mediums include painting, drawing, soon to be tattooing, and soon to be...coding. I also dabble in digital art as you can see here. My biggest inspiration for creating art resides in femininty, nature, and faces. My work often weaves these three themes together. In my spare time, I like to read, play with my puppy, and practice yoga. My ultimate dream is to be coding by day and tattooing by night. Both are amazing artforms that I am just beginning to dabble in!
